\subsection*{Prompt Variations}

\noindent \textbf{Original Prompt} (used for AudioFlamingo3\_original):
\begin{verbatim}
You are given an audio recording and a text statement.

Determine whether the text is consistent with the meaning conveyed in the audio.

Respond with one of the following labels only:
CONSISTENT
INCONSISTENT

STATEMENT:
{hypothesis}
\end{verbatim}

\noindent \textbf{Explicit Prompt (v2)} (used for AudioFlamingo3\_v2\_explicit):
\begin{verbatim}
Listen carefully to the audio recording. Then read the statement below.

Your task: Decide if the statement MATCHES or CONTRADICTS what is said in the audio.

- If the statement accurately reflects what is said in the audio, respond: CONSISTENT
- If the statement contradicts or misrepresents what is said in the audio, respond: INCONSISTENT

Think about what you heard in the audio before answering.

STATEMENT: {hypothesis}

Your answer (CONSISTENT or INCONSISTENT):
\end{verbatim}

\noindent \textbf{Simple Prompt (v3)} (used for AudioFlamingo3\_v3\_simple):
\begin{verbatim}
Audio statement verification task.

Does the following statement match what is said in the audio?

Statement: {hypothesis}

Answer with exactly one word - either CONSISTENT or INCONSISTENT:
\end{verbatim}
